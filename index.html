ℹ️ What's the difference between Issue and Defect?
⚠️
Issue — Minor, liveable Cosmetic or small functional problems. E.g. uneven paint, minor scratches, small tile gap. Builder can fix within 30 days after possession.
❌
Defect — Serious, act before signing Functional, structural or safety failures. E.g. water leakage, hollow tiles, broken flush, missing earthing, seepage. Do NOT sign possession papers until resolved. Get in writing from builder.
